About

I am a final year PhD student in the Computer Science department at Cornell University studying theoretical computer science. My research interests include complexity theory, pseudorandomness, and analysis of boolean functions. I am fortunate to be advised by Eshan Chattopadhyay. Previously, I was an undergrad at UC San Diego where I had the great pleasure to work with Russell Impagliazzo and Ramamohan Paturi.
