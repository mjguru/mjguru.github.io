Hi! I am a fourth year PhD student in the Computer Science department at Cornell University and am fortunate to be advised by Eshan Chattopadhyay. Previously, I was an undergrad at UC San Diego where I had the great pleasure to work with Russell Impagliazzo and Ramamohan Paturi.

I am primarily interested in theoretical computer science, specifically computational complexity and pseudorandomness.
